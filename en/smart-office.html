— FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Do you have any questions?

Here we have collected answers to the most frequently asked questions for commercial premises.

Contact us
Can you integrate new systems with our old equipment?
In most cases, yes. We work with flexible platforms that allow you to integrate existing cameras and sensors into new smart systems. Our specialists will assess the compatibility of your equipment on site.
Can I follow the office when I'm out of the country?
Undoubtedly. With our mobile and web apps, you can view cameras in real-time, check entry and exit, and receive alerts from anywhere with an internet connection.
Can we restrict employee access to certain rooms?
Yes. Our Access Control systems allow you to create access levels. For example, the server room can only be accessed by IT employees with a special card or fingerprint.
How does the system help save office costs?
Automating lighting and air conditioning, the system turns them off in rooms that are unoccupied or after hours. This can reduce energy costs by up to 20-30% per year.
