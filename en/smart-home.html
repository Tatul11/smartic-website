Contact us
How long does it take to install the system?
The duration of the installation depends on the complexity of the project and the size of the area. On average, it takes 1 to 3 weeks for the complete design and installation phases.
Can I manage the system from outside the home?
Yes, all our smart systems can be controlled from anywhere in the world using a dedicated mobile app and internet connection.
Are smart home systems safe?
We use systems that comply with international standards and have high encryption (for example, LifeSmart), which ensure maximum protection of your data and home from cyber attacks.
What happens if the power goes out?
Many devices (such as smart valves or security sensors) have built-in batteries. The central station of the system can be connected to an uninterruptible power supply (UPS), ensuring uninterrupted safety operation.
