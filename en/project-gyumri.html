Apartment in Gyumri, 120 square meters

Personalized smart apartment in Gyumri with automatic lighting and climate control.

Type: Housing:
Place: Shirak, Gyumri
Area: 120 sq.m.
Deadline: 2024

About the project

This project involved the complete automation of a modern two-story mansion. The client's main goal was to create an environment where security, climate control and lighting would work in sync and be controlled from one common dashboard or mobile app.

Our team started work during the construction phase, which allowed us to hide all the wires and sensors in the walls, keeping the minimalist interior design.

Basic requirements of the project

The client had clear requirements for home security and energy saving.

  • 360 degree exterior and interior video surveillance with night vision
  • Creating a smart heating and cooling system that adjusts according to the presence of people in the room
  • Remote control of gates and garage doors from the car or phone
  • Installation of water leakage and gas sensors with the possibility of automatic emergency shutdown
