— FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Everything you wanted to know about the smart home

Didn't find the answer? Call us for a free consultation.

Contact us
How much does a smart home system cost?

The cost depends on the surface of your apartment and the selected functions. We have starter packages ranging from simple lighting and security to premium solutions.

How long does installation take?

Installation usually takes 1 to 3 days depending on the complexity of the project. In the case of wireless systems, the work is done without damaging the repair.

Can I expand the system later?

Yes, the LifeSmart ecosystem is extremely flexible. You can start with just security sensors and later add lighting or climate control.

Does it work when the internet is down?

Yes, basic functions and scripts are stored in the Hub and work even without an Internet connection.

What warranty do you provide?

We provide up to 2 years official warranty for the equipment and free maintenance during the first year.
